New Event: The Mafia

Climb the ranks of the criminal underworld in this new event. Complete contracts to earn Rank EXP, Event Currency, and a chance to recruit powerful new allies.

The Ranks

There are a Total of 10 Ranks to climb, with each Rank unlocking new Contracts and Special Bonuses:

  • Boss
  • Underboss
  • Consigliere
  • Capo
  • Enforcer
  • Squad Leader
  • Elite Member
  • Member
  • Associate
  • Grunt

Contracts

Polpo will show a dropdown list of Contracts for you to do. For each rank there will be 3 Contracts available. Some Contracts are locked behind being a specific Rank.

Your Mafia Rank can be leveled up by completing contracts, with harder contracts giving more Rank EXP and Event Currency. You can also get Rank EXP from general play or extra missions added to your Daily/Weekly Quests. With each Rank you obtain, a new set of quests and rewards will be unlocked.
